TL;DR

A review taxonomy made inspectable and reusable

This atlas translates the review's conceptual axes into a transparent method-selection resource. Entries are curated from the literature and linked to primary publications and software resources where available, rather than generated as opaque recommendations.

Human-curated Classifications are maintained as a versioned table, not inferred at query time.
Assumption-aware Filters follow the review axes: data regime, mathematical design, supervision, and task.
Community-maintained Researchers can suggest updates through GitHub while curator review remains explicit.
